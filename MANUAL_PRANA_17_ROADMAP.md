## 📍 TIMELINE

### **FASE 1: CONSOLIDAÇÃO (JAN-MAR 2025)** ✅ IN PROGRESS

**Objetivo:** Solidificar features core, preparar para escala

**Key Milestones:**
- ✅ Dashboard com Sankalpa
- ✅ Energy/Mood integration
- ✅ Papyrus (Diary system)
- ✅ Ash basic (chat + insights)
- ⏳ Mobile app (iOS via Capacitor)
- ⏳ Performance optimization
- ⏳ Security audit + GDPR compliance

**Metrics:**
- 10k registered users
- 1k paying users (Pro)
- >85% app performance score

---

### **FASE 2: COLABORAÇÃO (ABR-JUN 2025)**

**Objetivo:** Habilitar trabalho em equipe sem perder foco pessoal

**Key Features:**
- **Team Projects**
  - Share projects with team members
  - Role-based access (owner, editor, viewer)
  - @mentions for collaboration
  - Comments on tasks/diary entries

- **Wellness Dashboard (Team)**
  - Anonymous mood distribution by team
  - Collective energy levels
  - Team productivity metrics
  - Burnout risk detection

- **Integrations**
  - Slack notifications
  - Google Workspace integration
  - Jira sync (for developers)
  - Asana import (data migration)

**Metrics:**
- 50k registered users
- 5k paying users
- 500 team accounts

---

### **FASE 3: ESPECIALIZAÇÃO (JUL-SET 2025)**

**Objetivo:** Criar agentes especializados que exploram Prana como Hub

**Key Agents:**
- **Coach Agent**
  - Habit formation guidance
  - Goal tracking + accountability
  - Coaching conversations (weekly check-ins)
  - Progress visualization

- **Content Agent**
  - Outline generation for writers
  - Email campaign suggestions
  - Social media content ideas
  - SEO optimization tips

- **Analyst Agent**
  - Deep data analysis (your life data)
  - Predictive insights ("You'll likely have energy spike next Tuesday")
  - Anomaly detection
  - Custom report generation

- **Synchronizer Agent**
  - Calendar sync (Google, Outlook, Apple)
  - Activity tracking (Fitbit, Apple Health)
  - Social media activity logging
  - Email inbox management hints

**Metrics:**
- 100k registered users
- 10k paying users
- 1k+ team accounts
- 50 enterprise customers

---

### **FASE 4: ECOSYSTEM (OUT-DEZ 2025)**

**Objetivo:** Prana como open platform

**Key Initiatives:**
- **API-First Architecture**
  - Public SDK (JavaScript, Python, Go)
  - Webhook system
  - Third-party agent development

- **Agent Marketplace**
  - Community creates specialized agents
  - Revenue share model (70/30)
  - Agent certification program
  - Featured agents section

- **Data Portability**
  - Export all data as JSON/CSV
  - Vault file format (encrypted)
  - Migration tools to other systems
  - Archive forever locally

- **White-Label Option**
  - Wellness companies use Prana as infrastructure
  - Custom branding
  - White-label pricing

**Metrics:**
- 200k registered users
- 25k paying users
- 10k team accounts
- $5M+ ARR

## ⚖️ PRINCIPLES GUIDING OUR ROADMAP

```
✅ DO:
- Listen to users, especially paying users
- Build one feature deeply rather than many shallowly
- Maintain zero tracking/surveillance
- Keep onboarding simple (5 min to first value)
- Move fast on feedback iterations

❌ DON'T:
- Become everything to everyone
- Sacrifice privacy for features
- Add complexity without solving real problems
- Ignore competitor moves (but don't chase them)
- Forget why Prana exists (holistic life OS)
```
