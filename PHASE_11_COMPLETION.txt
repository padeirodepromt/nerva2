## 🎓 Key Learnings This Phase

1. **ENERGIA ≠ ESTADO**
   - Energy = How to deposit work (mode/type)
   - Emotional State = Pure feeling/emotion
   - Both needed for complete picture

2. **Triple Check-in Elegance**
   - Morning (6am): Primary energy setup
   - Afternoon (2pm): Energy change detection
   - Evening (8pm): Reflexive emotional context
   - Patterns emerge from this sequence

3. **Intensity Scale Matters**
   - 1-5 provides right granularity
   - Not overwhelming user
   - Rich enough for detection algorithms

4. **Linking Creates Intelligence**
   - Morning energy → Afternoon update → Evening reflection
   - Complete day narrative
   - Basis for ritual detection

---

## 🎯 Why This Design Works

✨ **User Experience**
- Quick 30-second check-ins (morning/afternoon)
- Reflexive journaling (evening)
- Contextual emotional capture
- No friction, high signal

🧠 **Data Quality**
- 3-point data per day (morning, afternoon, evening)
- Linked across day
- Emotional context for energy
- Rich for ML/pattern detection

🔮 **Future Intelligence**
- Ritual detection from patterns
- Energy optimization suggestions
- Mood correlation analysis
- Personalized insights via Ash
